=head1 NAME

rum_runner status - Show the status of a job

=head1 SYNOPSIS

rum_runner status -o dir

=head1 DESCRIPTION

Show the status of any job, based on the output directory.

=head1 OPTIONS

=over 4

=item B<-o>, B<--output> I<dir>

Specify the output directory of the job. rum_runner can determine the
status of the job based on its output directory.

=back
